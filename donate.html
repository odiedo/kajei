Support St. Annes Kajei

Your generous contributions help St. Annes Kajei Junior and Secondary School in Amagoro Division, Teso North, Busia County, provide quality education to our 603 students (320 girls, 283 boys). By donating, you support our mission to deliver the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) and create a nurturing environment for academic and personal growth.

Every donation, big or small, directly impacts our students by funding infrastructure improvements, additional staff, and enriching programs. Join us in shaping the future of our community!
