Our Facilities

Supporting Holistic Education

At St. Anna, Kajei Comprehensive Schools in Amagoro Division, Teso North, Busia County, our facilities are designed to support the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) and foster a conducive learning environment for our 603 students (320 girls and 283 boys). While we are proud of our existing infrastructure, we are actively seeking support to enhance our facilities to meet the growing needs of our students.

Our facilities provide a foundation for academic excellence, extracurricular activities, and community engagement, ensuring every student thrives in a safe and inclusive environment.

Classrooms
18 classrooms for Pre-Primary, Primary, and Junior Secondary, equipped with basic learning aids to support CBC delivery.
Playground
A spacious playground for sports like football, volleyball, and athletics, fostering physical development and teamwork.
Library
A modest library with essential books and resources to support research and reading for all grades.
Dining Area
A dedicated space for students to enjoy meals, promoting nutrition and community interaction.
