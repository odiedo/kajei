About Us

Welcome to St. Annes Kajei Junior and Secondary School

Located in Amagoro Division, Teso North Sub-County, Busia County, St. Annes Kajei serves 603 pupils with a focus on quality education under the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC). Our school offers Pre-Primary, Primary, and Junior Secondary (Grades 7-9) education in a safe, inclusive environment.

Our mission is to provide skills-based education that empowers learners to thrive. Our vision is to be a model school in Busia County, excelling in academics, extracurriculars, and community service.
