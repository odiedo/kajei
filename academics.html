Excellence in CBC Education

Our Academic Approach

At St. Anna, Kajei Comprehensive Schools, located in Amagoro Division, Teso North, Busia County, we are committed to delivering a world-class Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) across our 18 classes, serving 603 students (320 girls and 283 boys). Our academic programs span Pre-Primary (PP1-PP2), Primary (Grades 1-6), and Junior Secondary (Grades 7-9), guided by 13 TSC-qualified teachers dedicated to fostering critical thinking, creativity, and practical skills.

Our CBC approach emphasizes hands-on learning, student-centered assessments, and holistic development. We aim to equip students with competencies for academic success and real-world challenges, supported by a nurturing and inclusive environment.

Academic Levels
  • Pre-Primary (PP1-PP2): Focus on play-based learning, language skills, and social development.
  • Primary (Grades 1-6): Core subjects include Mathematics, English, Kiswahili, Science, Social Studies, and Creative Arts.
  • Junior Secondary (Grades 7-9): Expanded curriculum with Integrated Science, Pre-Technical Studies, Business Studies, and optional subjects like Agriculture and Home Science.
