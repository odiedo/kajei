Admissions

Join St. Anna, Kajei Comprehensive Schools Today

At St. Anna, Kajei Comprehensive Schools, we welcome students from Pre-Primary (PP1-PP2) through Primary (Grades 1-6) to Junior Secondary (Grades 7-9). Located in Amagoro Division, Teso North, Busia County, our school offers a nurturing environment with a focus on the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC), fostering academic excellence, creativity, and holistic growth for our 603 students (320 girls and 283 boys).

Our admissions process is designed to be transparent and inclusive, ensuring every child has the opportunity to thrive. Contact us today to enroll your child or support our mission through scholarships and donations.

Admission Requirements
  • Birth certificate or age proof
  • Previous academic records (if applicable)
  • Completed application form (available at the school office)
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs
  • Transfer letter (for transferring students)
How to Apply

Visit our school office to collect the application form, or contact us below to schedule a visit. Submit the completed form with required documents to our admissions office. We conduct assessments for placement in Grades 1-9 to ensure the best learning fit.
